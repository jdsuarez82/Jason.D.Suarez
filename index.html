Brooke Loves American Idol

Brooke is a huge fan of American Idol. She has been watching the show since it first premiered and never misses an episode. She loves seeing the talented singers compete each week and enjoys rooting for her favorites. Brooke has even attended a few live tapings of the show and says it was one of the best experiences of her life. American Idol has become a big part of Brooke's life, and she can't imagine not having it to look forward to every week.
