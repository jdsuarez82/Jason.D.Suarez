Daisy: A Poem About a Korean Exchange Student

Daisy came from Seoul, so far away, With a smile that brightened each and every day. A Korean exchange student, full of grace, She brought a new perspective to our place.

She arrived with curiosity and wonder, Eager to learn and make friends like thunder. Her broken English, though a little shy, Couldn't hide her spirit that reached so high.

She marveled at the sights and sounds anew, The language, the food, the customs too. She shared her culture, her stories and more, Enriching our lives, down to the core.

Daisy was a gem, a diamond in the rough, With a heart so pure, and gentle as a puff. She faced challenges with courage and might, Overcoming obstacles, shining so bright.

She taught us about resilience and grace, About embracing differences and finding our place. Her laughter was infectious, her presence so sweet, With Daisy around, life was truly a treat.
