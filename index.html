Daisy: A Poem

Daisy from afar, a foreign land,
A Korean lass with dreams so grand,
Her journey took her across the sea,
To study in a land unknown, but free.

With a heart full of courage and grace,
She ventured into a new place,
Leaving behind her home and kin,
Embracing the unknown with a determined grin.

In a foreign land, she found her way,
Navigating through each new day,
Learning language, culture, and more,
Her spirit shining, her heart pure.

She made new friends, shared laughter and tears,
Her presence brightened, dispelling any fears,
Her smiles contagious, her kindness pure,
Daisy, a gem, for all to adore.

With time, she grew, blossoming like a flower,
Gaining wisdom and strength with each passing hour,
Leaving indelible memories, a cherished part,
Daisy, a soul that touched many a heart.

As her journey continued, the time flew by,
A Korean exchange student with wings to fly,
Leaving with memories, cherished and sweet,
A tale of courage, a journey complete.

Daisy, a name that will never fade,
A story of courage, a beautiful serenade,
A shining example of strength and grace,
A Korean exchange student who left her trace.
